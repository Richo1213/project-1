COVID is a drag, but with The ArtMapper, the latest in COVIDity apps, it is more bearable. ArtMapper allows you to visit galleries on your phone, one day throughout the world, but for now at the Harvard Art Galleries, thanks to their OpenSource API. You can explore works by topic, and save favourites. You can also explore cities and see where their galleries are and; when this COVID stuff is behind us, you can see which galleries are nearby. A must have for art enthusiasts, art thieves and anyone looking for inspiration. Stay tuned for more from ArtMapper.
